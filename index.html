About

Writer, storyteller and content creator, Jane Simataa is passionate about crafting narratives and drawing hearts in. Born of the Namibian soil, Jane weaves together culture, faith and transformation. She carries a background in media and communication with her working spanning over social media marketing, creative and academic essays and blog posts. Jane is the voice behind Believe and Beyond, a blog where she explores themes of identity, healing, hope and especially personal growth.
